About Charades Generator

We believe the best moments happen when the phone goes down and the laughter starts. That's why we built this.

Our Story

Charades Generator started with a simple frustration: every time we'd gather for game night, someone would forget to bring a charades word list. We'd spend the first ten minutes scrolling through phones or arguing over what categories to use instead of actually playing. Sound familiar?

So we built something better. A free, instant charades generator that loads in seconds and has something for everyone — from easy words for the little ones to genuinely hard phrases that'll stump the adults. No app download required. No signup. Just open it, pick a category, and start the fun.

What started as a weekend project has grown into a site used by families, teachers, event planners, and party hosts all over the world. We're proud of that, and we're committed to keeping it free, fast, and genuinely useful.

Who We Are

Charades Generator is built and maintained by a small team of people who genuinely love party games. We're designers, developers, and game night enthusiasts who got tired of bad user experiences in a category that deserves better.

We test every feature in real game nights with real families and friends. If something doesn't feel right when you're sitting around a living room trying to get a game going, we fix it. That's our quality bar.
