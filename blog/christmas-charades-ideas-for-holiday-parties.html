Tips for Christmas Charades Parties

Christmas charades works best when you mix categories across rounds. Don't play all songs in a row — alternate between movies, traditions, and food to keep energy high and give different people a chance to shine.

  • 🎅 Assign a Santa role — one person draws words for both teams and calls time. They don't play, they referee.
  • 🎵 For song titles, decide upfront whether humming the melody is allowed. It makes song rounds much faster and funnier.
  • 🍷 At adult parties, add a drinking game rule: if your team doesn't guess in time, everyone drinks.
  • 👧 For mixed ages, put the easy words in a separate pile for younger players so they're not stuck acting out "Figgy Pudding."
