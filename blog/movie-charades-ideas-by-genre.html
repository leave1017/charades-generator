Movie Charades Ideas by Genre – Action, Comedy, Horror & More

Movie charades is the best variant of the game. Everyone has seen enough films that even strangers can share a reference, and acting out a movie title creates those perfect moments of recognition. The key is picking titles that are actable — short enough to break into parts, distinctive enough to guess from gestures alone.

Acting Tips for Movie Titles

Movie titles are uniquely tricky because you need to simultaneously signal "this is a movie" and act out the specific title. Here's what works:

  • 🎬 Always start with the movie camera crank signal so your team knows it's a film.
  • 🔢 Hold up fingers for the number of words, then work through each word separately.
  • 👂 Use "sounds like" for tricky words. "Inception" → act like you're in + something being conceived.
  • 🌟 Act out the most visual element first. For "Titanic," mime a sinking ship — forget about acting the word "titanic."
  • ⏭ Skip "the," "a," "of" — they rarely help. Focus on the distinctive nouns and verbs.
