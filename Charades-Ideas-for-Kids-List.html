How to choose the right charades words for kids

  • Match the category to what they know. Young children do best with animals and simple everyday actions. Older kids enjoy characters from their favourite films and shows. When in doubt, ask what they're watching or playing right now.
  • Adjust the time limit, not the word. Rather than giving easier words to younger kids, give them more time. A 3-minute timer removes pressure and lets them work through the performance at their own pace.
  • Mix difficulty across a family game. Give younger players shorter, simpler words and older players the harder ones. Using our kids charades generator, you can switch categories between turns to keep every age group engaged.
